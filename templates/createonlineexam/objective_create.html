Help To Create Objective Exam

Please fill all the values asked on the first page, as they all are mandatory fields and you will not allow to move to the second page.

Your Email id will be saved and use for communication purpose, so be sure you are providing the correct email id.

Exam name is the name which will appear as the heading of the exam to the student and it will also use in future to search exam by name.

We store phone number for future preference.

After filling all these details click on the NEXT button it will direct you to the main exam creation page.

This page will have 10 questions and each question will have 4 options which will be given as choice to the student.

This page also have one more field name Correct Answer. Here you need to write the correct answer. Please do not write the option number as it will give you the wrong result. You need to write the complete answer in Correct Answer filed.

Default time limit for the exam is 20 min. We are working on it, in future you will be allow to choose the time limit of the exam.This feature will come on the next release.

The result will be auto calculated by the system and the score will be send to you at your email address and you can also view all the result in result tab on the home page.

After successful Exam Creation, A Email with all the details will be sent to your email id.
